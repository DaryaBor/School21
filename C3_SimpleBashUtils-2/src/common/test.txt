The 'Harry Potter' series, penned by British author J.K. Rowling, 
has captivated readers and audiences around the world since the release of its first book, 
'Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone,' in 1997. Set in a richly imagined magical universe, 
the story follows the journey of an orphaned boy, Harry Potter, who discovers on his eleventh birthday 
that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he meets 
lifelong friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger and is introduced to a world filled with spell-casting,
 mythical creatures, and intense rivalries.
